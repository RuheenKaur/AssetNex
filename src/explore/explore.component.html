WHY AssetNexIT?

Modern Asset Management : Simplifies tracking, assignment, and status monitoring of IT assets across departments.

E-Waste Disposal : Compliance Ensures responsible and trackable e-waste management aligned with institutional policies.

Reduces Manual Work : Automates asset logs, request workflows, and support tickets—minimizing paperwork.

Secure Access Control : Role-based login for admins and users ensures authorized access to sensitive data.
Key Functionalities

🧾 Asset : Inventory Add, update, view, or retire hardware/software assets in a centralized dashboard. 🛠️ Support Request System: Users can log issues with assets; admins can track, respond, and resolve them. 🔄 E-Waste Disposal Requests: Submit old or non-functional assets for approval and safe disposal. Role-Based Authentication : Master admins get full access; users see only their relevant data and forms. 📊 Dashboard Insights : Visual summaries of asset usage, ticket trends, and disposal activity. 🔄 Software License Tracking : Monitor software renewals, expiry dates, and license allocations.
